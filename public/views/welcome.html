Welcome to GISTYLE!

GISTYLE is a an application made as a project in the course Programming in geomatics at NTNU.

With GISTYLE you can both do geographical analysis, and also style your map to visualize the information the way you want.

A good way to get started is to do some tutorials, which you can find in the top menu once logged in.
